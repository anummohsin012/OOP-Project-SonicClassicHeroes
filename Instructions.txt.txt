Use Arrow keys to move sideways

Use Space key to jump

Use 'z' key to change characters

Collect all the coins in the time limit for the door to open and take you
to the next level!!

Collect extra lives for durability and boosters to unlock special 
abilities for a short duration!

Special Abilities:
Sonic: Speed+4
Tails: Flying ability
Knuckles: Invincibility
